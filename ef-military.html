Military Effects

• The Lockheed Have Blue project paved the way for future stealth planes, such as F117-a, F35a Lighting, B-2 and B-21 Bombers.


• There are approximately 202 stealth fighter and bomber planes in the U.S. Air Force's current inventory, with the true number being unkown as the information is currently classified.


• The technology pioneered by the Have Blue project is being used by many different nations to make their own stealth planes, such as the U.S., China, and Russia.
