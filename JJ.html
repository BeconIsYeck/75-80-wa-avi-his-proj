Jumbo Jets

• The Boeing 747, Boeing largest plane at that time, was first made in 1969 in Everett, Washington, with varients being made throughout the 1970s and later.

A newer model of Boeing 747 that is one of Lufthansa Airlines' four hundred airplanes taking off at an airport in Frankfurt, Germany circa 2019. This specific plane is landing in Bosnia and Herzegovina.


• The 747 was made due to the increase in passenger air traffic in the 50s and 60s, with it being designed to be more fuel efficient and simpler larger than previous designs.


• It is 1.5x longer and wider than its predecessor, the Boeing 737, can carry four times the amount of passengers, with the 747 being able to hold around 400 people, intead of the 737's around 100 people.
