Economical Effects

• Since 1,568 Boeing 747s have been ordered in total, if we price each plane to be around $150,000,000 that means that roughly $235,100,000,000 worth of 747s have been sold in the past 50 years.


• If we set price of manufactoring each plane at a sligher smaller yet still similar amount, that would mean that about $200,000,000,000 has been spent manufactoring all of these planes.


• All of this money has tremendously helped the economy in Washington, since all of this revenue is helping support individual people and companies, both big and small.
