Societal Effects

• Boeing 747 planes have been used across the world by countless different countires, such as, France, China, Japan, Egypt, Australia, and Brazil, connecting people from all over the world.


• In the 1970s, there was a large increase in passenger air traffic even more so than the increase in the 50s-60s, which corelates to the new advancements in commerical aircraft, showing the impacts of the 747 and its relatives.


• The 747 would lead the way for many other jumbo jets in the future, such as later Boeing models, such as the 767 and 777, as well as Airbus planes, like the Airbus A380.
