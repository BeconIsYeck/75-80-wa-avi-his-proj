Boeing E-3 Sentry

• The Boeing E-3 Sentry was a surveillance plane made by Boeing in 1977, with it being tested later that same year in Oklahoma, and was designed for general military and police usage.

The Boeing E-3 Sentry was a surveillance plane made by Boeing in 1977, with it being tested later that same year in Oklahoma, and was designed for general military and police usage.


• The plane is built off of the Boeing 707's design and body, because of its great fuel efficiency at the time, but with the inclusion of an AWACS (Airborne Warning And Control System) design.


• Its AWACS design uses an highly advanced AEW&C (Airborne Early Warning & Control) radar for its time, making it one of the best airplane radars at that time, with it still holding up today as a effective and reliable sentry plane.
